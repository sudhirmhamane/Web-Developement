Cricket History

Cricket originated in England during the 16th century, becoming a popular sport by the 18th century.
The first international match was played in 1844 between the USA and Canada.The sport's growth spread globally,
with Test cricket beginning in 1877.Today, it's played in various formats, including T20 and ODIs, with millions of fans worldwide.
